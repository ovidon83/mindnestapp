# Disable Email Confirmation in Supabase (For Development)

To sign up and immediately use the app without email confirmation:

1. Go to your Supabase project dashboard: https://supabase.com/dashboard/project/uzwyovtwyltfjrswgtct
2. Click on **Authentication** in the left sidebar
3. Click on **Settings** (or go to Authentication â†’ Settings)
4. Scroll down to **Email Auth** section
5. Find **"Enable email confirmations"** toggle
6. **Turn it OFF** (disable it)
7. Save the changes

After disabling email confirmation:
- Users can sign up and immediately sign in
- No confirmation email will be sent
- Perfect for development and testing

**Note:** Re-enable email confirmation before deploying to production for security.
